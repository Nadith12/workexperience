The Action
For the EIF subject, I am tasked with finding a workplace where I will work for a week to gain experience. Since this assignment was given to all Year 10 students, I seized the opportunity to complete both tasks simultaneously.
This presentation outlines the journey of this task and highlights the valuable experience I gained. This experience will be beneficial for my future job-related activities, such as creating a resume, researching companies, seeking help, and building confidence for interviews.
I would like to thank my teachers and parents for their support, which enabled me to complete this task and gain more experience than I had anticipated.

Reflection
I have learned how to create a resume, enabling me to craft my own in the future to present to potential employers. Additionally, I have acquired skills in finding suitable job opportunities, which will assist me in securing a workplace in the future. This includes understanding how to perform in interviews, create resumes, search for appropriate positions, and even coding in HTML and CSS.
Creating my resume was a successful experience, as I received valuable feedback and ideas from my teachers, who shared insights from their own resumes. However, I realized I could improve my eye contact and how to conclude my responses during an interview.
For assistance in finding a workplace, I sought help from Ms. Povey, who guided me in identifying suitable opportunities.
